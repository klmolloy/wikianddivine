"I've Got No Reason To Be Afraid Any More."

Release Date: December 17, 2014

Act two picks up a month after Luci's death. The world has moved on. Laura hasn't. She has been able to speak about it, she has been able to replicate lighting her cigarette with a snap of her fingers and she's become famous without saying a word. It's in the middle of this that Laura gets a mysterious call setting up a meet. The caller turns out to be Inanna. He tells her that he has found out that the would-be-assassins of Lucifer were fans, he tells her he doesn't trust the rest of the Pantheon anymore and he asks Laura to keep looking into what happened. After Inanna's vist Laura tweets that she is ready to talk for anyone who wants her.
